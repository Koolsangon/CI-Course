Content Fundamentals

Training slides hold a "warm authority" tone. The system reflects LG's 2023 rebrand values: "Human-centered Innovation" and "Warmth to Power a Smile."

Principles

  • Polite Korean verb endings (~합니다 / ~입니다) by default. All-hands audience, respectful register
  • Address as "여러분" ("everyone"), not "당신/you" — evokes collective learning
  • Arabic numerals with units: 3 ways, 2025, 12 months. Sino-Korean numerals only on cover / in quotes
  • Code-switch only for proper nouns — LG, OLED, HRD, AI. Common English words should be written in Hangul
  • Titles are noun phrases; body is complete sentences. No periods in titles
  • CTAs are verbs — Start, Learn more, Contact. Avoid exclamation marks
  • No emoji. Use icons instead

Good copy example

Title: 2025 Core-Values Internalization (2025년 핵심가치 내재화)
Subtitle: 3 behavior principles that change your day-to-day
Body: The way we work shapes customer experience. This session walks through three concrete behaviors with on-the-ground examples.

Copy to avoid

  • "Level UP your career! 🚀" — mixed English, emoji, informal
  • "7 tips you NEED to know!!" — hype, exclamation marks
  • "Next Level Leadership" — literal English title in a Korean context

  • LG Red — cap at roughly 10% of any screen. Never as a wide fill
  • Active Red — digital accent: interactive elements, chart highlights, Live badges
  • Neutral scale #FAFAFA → #0A0A0A, 8 steps. Enough for all backgrounds, cards, and text
  • Semantic — Success #2E7D32, Warning #ED6C02, Info #1565C0, Error = Active Red

Typography

  • UI / body: Pretendard Variable (LG Smart substitute) — balanced Hangul, Latin, and numerals
  • Scale: Display 72 / H1 48 / H2 36 / H3 28 / Body-L 22 / Body 18 / Caption 14
  • Minimum 24px on 16:9 slides
  • Line-height — titles 1.15, body 1.55, long-form 1.7
  • Letter-spacing — Hangul body -0.01em, large titles -0.03em

Backgrounds

  • Default — pure white #FFFFFF or off-white #FAFAFA
  • Section dividers — full-bleed LG Red or near-black, white typography
  • Allowed gradient: Active Red → LG Red diagonal only (cover / section dividers). No purple or blue gradients
  • Pattern: assets/pattern-dotgrid.svg at 5% opacity maximum

Motion

  • Easing: cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.8, 0.2, 1) — quick start, soft settle
  • Duration: 150ms (buttons) / 250ms (cards) / 400ms (slide transitions)
  • All transitions = fade + translate ≤ 8px. No bounce, rotation, or scale > 1.05

States

  • Hover — background 5% darker, or text underline
  • Press — transform: scale(0.98) + background 10% darker
  • Focus — 2px Active Red outline + 2px white inner ring, 2px offset
  • Disabled — 40% opacity, cursor not-allowed

Shape & Elevation

  • Radius: XS 4 / S 8 / M 12 / L 16 / XL 24 / Pill 999. Slide cards default to L
  • Border: 1px --border-subtle; 2px for emphasis
  • 4 shadow steps (xs · sm · md · lg), all neutral gray. No colored shadows

Layout — 16:9

  • Safe area: 1920 × 1080, 80px margin → inner content 1760 × 920
  • Grid: 12 columns, 32px gutter
  • Fixed elements — LG logo bottom-left (32px tall), page number bottom-right N / Total
  • No glassmorphism or backdrop-blur (clarity first for training material)

Iconography

  • Lucide Icons (CDN, MIT) as a substitute — 1.5 stroke, rounded line caps
  • Sizes: 20 / 24 / 32 / 48. Body on slides: 28–32
  • Color inherits currentColor, single-tone only. No multi-color icons
  • Brand symbol: assets/lg-logo.svg is a symbolic reproduction. Replace with the official vector
  • No emoji or decorative Unicode. Only math symbols (× ÷ ≤ ≥) and check (✓) are exempt

Tweakable Knobs

  • theme — light / dark / lg-red
  • density — comfortable / compact
  • accent — LG Red / LG Active Red
  • logoPosition — bottom-left / top-left / hidden
